About Bursa IPO Tracker

Empowering retail investors with transparent, data-driven, and mathematical analysis of Bursa Malaysia IPOs.

Our Mission

Bursa Malaysia's Initial Public Offering (IPO) market is dynamic, yet retail investors often struggle with complex prospectuses, biased broker reports, and emotional listing day hype. Our mission is to democratize institutional-grade market data, providing clear, unbiased mathematical filters to separate high-conviction plays from high-risk ventures.

What We Do

We compile and enrich real-time and historical IPO listings across five stages—from exposure (draft prospectus) to listing and secondary market trading. Through our unique, mathematical valuation matrix (often called **Cincai2 Kira**), we calculate conservative target prices, historical oversubscription trends, and sector comparisons.

Mathematical Rigor Over Hype

We believe in strict discipline. We grade IPOs (Grade A, B, C) based on objective metrics: retail demand (Oversubscription ratio), valuation (Price-to-Earnings compared to sector peers), financial health (operating cash flows), and underwriter track records. We help retail players avoid "full allocation" traps and protect their capital.

Our Expertise (E-E-A-T)

Bursa IPO Tracker is developed and maintained by a group of retail market analysts and quantitative finance enthusiasts. Collectively, we have tracked and participated in over 100+ listings in Bursa Malaysia.

Please note that we are not registered investment advisers (Ria) or licensed brokers. All analysis presented here is strictly for research and mathematical modeling purposes.
