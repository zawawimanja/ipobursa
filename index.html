Grade A

SWING

Main Market / GLC giants. High institutional support. Goal: Hold for institutional accumulation throughout Day 1.

  • OS: > 20x
  • PE: Stable (< 15x)
Pro Tip: Tech/AI sectors. If OS is high, hold until 5:00 PM close.

Grade B

SCALP

ACE Market growth plays. High retail hype. Goal: Capture the initial morning spike and exit within 30 mins.

  • OS: > 50x (Required)
  • Sector: DC / Solar / Technology
Rule: Sell between 9:00 AM – 9:05 AM. Hype fades quickly.

Grade C

AVOID

Low interest/high valuation. Warning: High risk of "Full Allocation" where you get 100% of requested shares.

  • OS: < 10x
  • PE: Overpriced (> 20x)
Danger: If full allocation happens, sell immediately at opening.

Main Market vs ACE Market Comparison

🏛️ Main Market (MM)
  • Established, big companies
  • High profit track record required
  • Generally safer for long-term holds
⚡ ACE Market
  • Fast-growing, smaller companies
  • High growth & scalability potential
  • Higher volatility & retail hype

The "Cheat Sheet" to IPO Metrics

Fair Value (FV)

Analysts' estimated actual worth. Look for FV > Offer Price.

P/E Ratio

The price tag per profit. Lower P/E than sector is attractive.

Oversubscription (OS)

Retail demand multiplier. Higher OS indicates high retail demand.

Market Capitalization

Total company value. Large caps are stable; small caps grow faster.
